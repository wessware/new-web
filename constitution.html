The Ammended Set Kenya Constitution, 2018

The Constitution


June, 2021

ARTICLE I: NAME OF THE ASSOCIATION
The full name of the union shall be Students Empowerment Team Kenya, hereby known and referred to as SET Kenya.
ARTICLE II: OBJECTIVES OF THE ASSOCIATION
The objectives of the association shall include;-

  1. To reach out to young people in high schools and to communities around on the importance of tertiary education specifically universities.
  2. To ensure the five counties of western Kenya and their environs natures talent and resources through providing training and empowerment to the region
  3. To offer Christian based career talks and peer counseling in high schools in the region
  4. To solicit for funds through the right channels to enable the union fulfill its objectives.

ARTICLE III: MEMBERSHIP
  1. Students affiliated to Western Outreach (WESO), a subset of the JKUAT CU are all bona fide members of SET Kenya.
  2. Any other person shall be a member if and only if his/her membership is approved by the executive committee if the above (I) does not apply.
  3. Alumni members shall have associate membership to the union.

ARTICLE IV: OFFICE BEARERS
The association shall have an executive committee that shall oversee the daily running of the union. The executive committee shall have seven office bearers namely;-
  1. Chairperson
  2. Vice chairperson
  3. Secretary
  4. Treasurer
  5. Organizing secretary
  6. Publicity secretary
  7. Projects coordinator

ARTICLE V: DUTIES OF THE OFFICIALS
DUTIES OF THE EXECUTIVE COMMITTEE
  1. The committee shall oversee the running and implementation of the vision, mission and objectives of the group
  2. Determining the next venue for a mission
  3. Training of members in preparation for the various missions

5.1. DUTIES OF OFFICIALS
1. CHAIRPERSON
The chairperson shall;
  1. Preside over all meetings i.e. general meetings and those of the committee
  2. Foster the realization of the vision mission and objectives of the group.
  3. Be a signatory to the groups bank account.
  4. Coordinate the works of the other committee members.

2. VICE CHAIRPERSON
The vice chairperson shall;
  1. Perform the duties of the chairperson in his/her absence
  2. Oversee organization of trainings of the members and the committee
  3. Oversee keeping and distribution of the groups literature materials

3. SECRETARY
The secretary shall:
  1. Record the minutes of the committee and general group meetings.
  2. Deal with all the group correspondence
  3. Keep a record of all group membership.

4. ORGANIZING SECRETARY
The organizing secretary shall;
  1. Oversee correspondence to the various organizations or institutions for missions.
  2. Secure of venue for meetings and activities
  3. Oversee organization of transport to various mission grounds

5. TREASURER
The treasurer shall;
  1. Receive all the moneys and funds and shall deposit such in the bank approved by the committee
  2. Ensure that proper records are kept of all the money received and are available for inspection
  3. Keep, safeguard and update all the records and all the assets of the group.

6. PUBLICITY SECRETARY
The publicity secretary shall;
  1. Ensure that the activities of the union are well publicized
  2. Ensure flow of information to students and associates
  3. Be in charge of the twitter and face book accounts of the group

7. PROJECTS COORDINATOR
The projects coordinator shall;
  1. Be in charge of the groups projects, manage and access the feasibility of such project
  2. Provide such information regarding projects to the executive committee

ARTICLE VI: ELECTION OF OFFICIALS
  1. There shall be established an electoral college to run the election of new officials
  2. The Electoral College shall constitute of at least seven non- eligible members
  3. Elections shall be carried out annually by all registered members of SET.
  4. Prior to election, members shall nominate some of the eligible members in various dockets.
  5. Elections shall be carried out one week before the annual general meeting.

ARTICLE VII: TERM OF OFFICE OF OFFICIALS
  1. The elected members shall be in office for one year
  2. The term shall run from the time of their election to the time of the next election

ARTICLEVIII: ACTIVITIES OF THE ASSOCIATION
The activities of the association shall include;
  1. Visiting schools and giving talks to students in schools in the western region and other regions as shall be required.
  2. Carrying out activities such as carwash within the campus
  3. Visiting children homes and carrying out community works

ARTICLE IX: MEETINGS OF THE ASSOCIATION
  1. Meetings of the association shall be after every fortnight
  2. Extra ordinary meetings can be called by the chairperson in case of an urgent matter requiring attention of the members

ARTICLE X: COMMITEES OF THE ASSOCIATION
  1. The association shall have the executive committee that shall consist of all the elected officials
  2. The association shall have special committees to handle specific activities of the association
  3. Membership of the special committees shall be voluntary and shall have a maximum of seven members

ARTICLE XI: FUNDS OF THE ASSOCIATION
The funds of the association shall comprise of-
  1. Subscription from members.
  2. Donations, gifts and grants.
  3. Such monies or assets as may accrue to or vest in the association in the course of the exercise of its powers or the performance of its functions under this Constitution
  4. All monies from any other sources provided for or lent to the association.
The funds of the association may be used for the following purposes;
  1. Recurrent expenses;
  2. Capital development;
  3. To facilitate furtherance of the associations objectives

ARTICLE XII: CODE OF CONDUCT
Members of SET shall conduct themselves in a mature manner in the process of carrying out the objectives and activities of the association.
ARTICLE XIII: AMENDMENT OF THE CONSTITUTION
Amendments to this Constitution may be proposed by two methods;
  1. Such a proposal shall not be considered unless it has been seconded and supported by at least two thirds of the members and 2 weeks in advance. The proposed amendment should also be submitted in writing to the Executive Committee who shall submit such amendments for debate at a General Meeting.
  2. Amendment shall only be adopted if such a proposal is supported by two thirds of the SET members. This amendment shall invalidate any provision inconsistent with it.

ARTICLE XIV: DISSOLUTION OF THE ASSOCIATION
The association shall be dissolved upon agreement by the members.
ARTICLE XV: OFFICE PATRON
The association shall have a patron The patron shall;
  1. They shall at their own pleasure attend meetings of the association with the status of an observer.
  2. Provide advice or any other technical support required by the association.
