The Patron

The Executive committee oversees the running of the Organization; implementation of the vsion, mission and objectives of the group. This team is also responsible for training members of the Organization in preparation for various missions which they too, deliberate on the next venue.

The Patron - Mr. Daniel Nyongesa, oversees the running of the Organization as an ex-official member of the executive committee. He attends meetings of the Executive committee at his own will, with the status of an observer. He also provides any other technical support required by the association.
