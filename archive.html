SET KENYA NEWS

JKUSA clubs fair 2020


Monday November 24, 2020
In November 2020, Set Kenya joined other clubs within the University to showcase and exihibit their services to the new students who joined duiring the September 2020 intake.
This being an annual initiative by JKUSA - Jomo Kenyatta University Students Association, Set Kenya did participate for the first time since registration as a club in 2017. The event was a great success both for JKUSA and Set Kenya alike. Gracing the occasion was the University Vice Chancellor, Professor Victoria Wambui Ngumi. She reitarated on the importance of unity in cultural diversity within the JKUAT student fraternity.
Set Kenya through its showcase, was able to recruit a good number of new members, whose details were captured for orientation and induction to the organization.
